Split your daily expenses

with fellow students

Post

Want to get some grub on the cheap? Pitch your idea, assemble with other willing students, get your food and save big in order to do it all over again next time.

Contribute

Pitch in to share the cost of a fellow student's purchase and get a share of it. From concert tickets, renting a place, to living essentials, anything can be a reason to contribute.

Community

Pitch is all about building a tightly-knit community on campus. Explore your city with students you would have never met. Make new connections, contribute together and save money while you're at it.
